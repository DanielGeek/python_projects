# Banoffee Pie Recipe

An easy family favourite with buttery pastry and sweet dulce de leche. Recommended to serve with a generous dollop of cream.

## Serves
8 - 10

## Difficulty
Easy

## Prep Time
30 minutes

## Cook Time
30 - 35 minutes

## Ingredients

### For the filling
- 4 bananas, sliced
- 394g caramel or dulce de leche
- 300ml double cream
- Dark chocolate (optional)

### For the pastry
- 100g butter, chilled (plus extra for greasing)
- 200g plain flour
- 1 medium egg, separated
- 1 tbsp golden caster sugar

## Method

1. Make the pastry case: Put the butter and flour in a food processor and pulse until it resembles fresh breadcrumbs. Add the egg yolk and sugar, pulse again until mixed. Add very cold water a tablespoon at a time, pulsing after each. Stop when dough forms one piece.
2. Remove dough and gently knead by hand until smooth. Wrap tightly in cling film and chill for 30 minutes.
3. Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 4. Grease a 23cm loose-bottomed fluted tart tin. Roll out pastry and line the tin, leaving about half a cm proud of the edge. Snip to length.
4. Line base with baking parchment, weigh down with baking beans or uncooked rice. Bake for 15 minutes. Remove paper and beans, brush pastry with beaten egg white. Bake for another 15-20 minutes until crisp and golden. Cool completely.
5. Spread half the caramel on the pastry case. Layer sliced bananas on top. Cover with the remaining caramel and refrigerate.
6. Whip cream until thick and billowy, spoon over tart. Grate dark chocolate over to serve (optional).

Enjoy your delicious Banoffee Pie!
